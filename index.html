Our Story

Baked with Purpose.
Shared with Love.

Founded in California in 2004 and bringing our craft to Seattle in 2011, Brown Sugar Baking Company is the city's only specialty organic, nut-free wholesale and retail baked goods company.

Owner Lillian Hill — wife, mother, daughter, community leader — built this bakery on a simple but powerful belief: everyone deserves a birthday cake. Whether you have extreme food allergies or strict dietary restrictions, you shouldn't have to miss out on life's sweetest moments.

We source locally, employ from the immediate neighborhood, and bake every item with the care and artistry of traditional techniques.
